AI-Driven Gamification in Higher Education - Group Review Paper

One of my key academic projects was a group review paper titled "AI-Driven Gamification Platforms in Higher Education." I was responsible for writing the Introduction and Abstract, where I set the context by discussing limitations of traditional teaching methods and introduced how AI and gamification together can create personalized, engaging learning experiences. The paper explores Intelligent Tutoring Systems, adaptive learning, and student motivation frameworks.

Authors:Shreyas Rajesh Nair,Harith Bhuvan S, Tulasi Lasya Gorrela, Kashyap S, Pallapothu Hanumath Sai Krishna

Affiliation: Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amritapuri, India

Year: 2025 S1
